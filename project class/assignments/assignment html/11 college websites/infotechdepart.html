Information technology Department

The Information Technology Department (ITD) aims to provide meaningful impact through smart uses of technology in service of employees and the community. IT executes and sustains vital City services through business solutions, cyber security, and the Customer Contact Center. Services are powered by robust infrastructure and operations which include an expansive municipal area network, compute and storage, and enterprise productivity and collaboration tools. IT provides citywide Strategic IT Planning, administers the Enterprise Architecture and product-portfolio management, fiscal and billing services.
