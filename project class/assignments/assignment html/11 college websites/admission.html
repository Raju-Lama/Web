Admission Process

Welcome to the University of Hogwarts admission page! We are excited to guide you through the process of joining our esteemed institution.

Steps to Apply:

  1. Visit our application portal.
  2. Fill out the online application form with your personal details.
  3. Submit your academic transcripts and letters of recommendation.
  4. Complete the entrance examination (details available on the portal).
  5. Attend an interview with our admissions committee.
  6. Receive your admission decision via email.
  7. If accepted, follow the instructions to confirm your enrollment.

Important Dates:

  • Application Deadline: June 30, 2025
  • Entrance Examination: July 15, 2025
  • Interviews: July 20-25, 2025
  • Admission Decisions: August 1, 2025
  • Enrollment Confirmation Deadline: August 15, 2025
